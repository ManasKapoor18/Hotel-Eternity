Welcome To Our Hotel

"A guest is the most important visitor on our premises. He is not dependent on us.
We are dependent on him. He is not an interruption in our work. He is the purpose of
it. He is not an outsider in our business. He is part of it. We are not doing him a favor
by serving him. He is doing us a favor by giving us an opportunity to do so."

matter of great honor and pride for us.
